About Me

An economics student with a knack for programming. My rigorous education at the National University of Singapore, coupled with my internship experiences in the technology sector, has equipped me with a strong set of tools to go about making positive impact at scale.

I dipped my toes into programming after my first year in university and never looked back. Taking external courses, online courses, and working on data science projects during my free time, I developed a keen interest in solving problems with data.

Every week, I do my best to squeeze in some time to read. In particular, I enjoy reading biographies and books on technology. I'm also a huge Elon Musk fan and a HODLer 😜
